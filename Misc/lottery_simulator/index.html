There are 6 red balls and 1 blue ball
red number is from 1 to 33
blue is 1 to 16
each lottery costs $2
1st and 2nd prize money is determined by the pool

  • 1st: all nums match, according to history the average is around $6,000,000
  • 2nd: 6 red nums match, average is around $200,000
  • 3rd: 5 red, 1 blue, get fixed $3000
  • 4th: all 5 red or 4 red and 1 blue, get fixed $200
  • 5th: 4 red or 3 red and 1 blue, get fixed $10
  • 6th: 1 blue, get fixed $5


Each lottery is $2
It will pop up a alert window to notify you when you win 3rd prize or higher
reference: https://www.cwl.gov.cn/c/2018/10/12/417937.shtml and http://www.cwl.gov.cn/ygkj/wqkjgg/ssq/
